Elitra Health has launched a concierge primary care program, marking a significant step in its commitment to longevity and preventive medicine. Announced on March 20, 2026, the initiative builds on the company’s experience with over 5,000 Executive Exams conducted annually since 2016, positioning Elitra as a leading provider in the East Coast market.

This membership-based model offers a range of services, including 24/7 physician access, same-day appointments, and personalized wellness planning. By limiting physician panels to just 35 patients, Elitra aims to foster deeper patient-physician relationships and enhance continuity of care. The program also integrates advanced diagnostics and health monitoring, creating a comprehensive approach that contrasts with traditional high-volume health systems.

For longevity professionals, this model underscores the growing trend towards personalized, accessible healthcare that prioritizes patient engagement and preventive strategies, potentially reshaping how primary care is delivered in the context of aging and healthspan research.
