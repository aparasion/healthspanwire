Iradimed Corporation (NASDAQ: IRMD) is set to showcase its advancements in MRI-compatible medical devices at the upcoming 25th Annual Needham Virtual Healthcare Conference, scheduled for April 13-16, 2026. CFO Jack Glenn will engage in one-on-one meetings with investors, highlighting the company's unique position as the sole provider of a non-magnetic intravenous (IV) infusion pump system designed specifically for MRI environments.

The significance of Iradimed's innovations lies in their ability to enhance patient safety and treatment efficacy during MRI procedures. The **MRidium® MRI-compatible IV infusion pump** employs a non-magnetic ultrasonic motor and specially designed non-ferrous components, eliminating the risks associated with standard infusion pumps that can interfere with MRI technology. This system is particularly crucial for critically ill patients and pediatric populations requiring sedation, as it ensures uninterrupted delivery of vital medications during scans. Additionally, the **Iradimed 3880 MRI-compatible patient vital signs monitoring system** operates reliably in high magnetic fields, facilitating continuous monitoring of patients' vital signs without the risks posed by traditional equipment.

The implications of these developments are profound for both clinical practice and research in aging biology. By providing reliable and safe monitoring and medication delivery during MRI scans, Iradimed's technologies could shift current paradigms in patient management, particularly in vulnerable populations. This innovation may accelerate the integration of advanced imaging techniques in clinical settings, ultimately enhancing patient outcomes and informing ongoing research into aging and healthspan interventions. As such, Iradimed's contributions may play a pivotal role in advancing therapeutic protocols and improving the overall efficacy of care for aging patients.
