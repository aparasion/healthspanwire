**Longevity Day at NFC Summit Lisbon** is set to debut on **June 4, 2026**, at the Unicorn Factory, bringing together a diverse array of professionals from the longevity ecosystem, including scientists, clinicians, founders, and investors. Curated by Michelangelo Gallia and Nina Patrick, co-founders of Longevity Wednesdays, this event is integrated into the larger NFC Summit, one of Europe’s most significant tech and entrepreneurship conferences, which attracts around **4,000 attendees** from **59 countries**. 

The event is structured around three thematic pillars: **Wisdom from the Past**, **Knowledge from the Present**, and **Hope from the Future**. This framework allows for a comprehensive exploration of longevity, balancing ancestral practices with cutting-edge research. Confirmed speakers include notable figures such as **Diogo Barardo** from NOVOS and **Dr. Jeff Vogel** from Concorde Health, who will share insights on current advancements in longevity science. Additionally, speakers like **Francisco Marques-Teixeira** and **Caitlin Lewis** will discuss historical perspectives and future possibilities, ensuring a well-rounded dialogue on the subject.

The implication of Longevity Day extends beyond networking; it represents a significant opportunity for collaboration across various sectors of the longevity field. By convening such a diverse group of experts, the event aims to catalyze new partnerships and accelerate the translation of research into clinical applications. This could potentially shorten drug development timelines and enhance the integration of innovative therapies into healthcare practices, ultimately advancing the field of longevity science.
