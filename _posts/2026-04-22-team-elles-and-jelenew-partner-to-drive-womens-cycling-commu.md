**On March 28, 2026, Team Elles, a French developmental cycling team, collaborated with high-performance cycling apparel brand JELENEW to host a Rando Ride in the Loire Atlantique region. This event, coinciding with the French Cup race weekend, attracted nearly 200 female cyclists, ranging from amateurs to seasoned competitors, underscoring a commitment to inclusivity and growth within the sport.**

**The Rando Ride served as a strategic engagement platform for JELENEW, which provided high-performance cycling gear and branded kits to participants. This initiative not only showcased the brand's dedication to supporting female athletes but also highlighted the integration of advanced technical engineering with the evolving lifestyle of modern cycling. The collaboration aligns with Team Elles' mission to lower barriers for women and youth in cycling, following their successful 2024 season where they achieved top amateur rankings.**

**The key takeaway from this partnership is the potential shift in the cycling apparel market towards more inclusive designs tailored specifically for women. By prioritizing female-specific technical apparel, JELENEW and Team Elles are setting a precedent that could influence future product development and marketing strategies in the cycling industry. This focus on inclusivity may catalyze broader initiatives aimed at empowering women in sports, ultimately fostering a more diverse and robust cycling community.**
