Researchers found that a UK-based company, Nuchido, is taking a unique approach to restoring NAD+, a crucial molecule for cellular health. Instead of simply supplementing with NAD+ precursors, Nuchido focuses on enhancing the body's natural ability to produce this molecule, which tends to decline with age. Their flagship product, Nuchido TIME+, aims to reactivate the body's own repair mechanisms, offering a more sophisticated solution to aging than just topping up levels of NAD+.

This matters for anyone interested in aging well. By improving NAD+ levels, Nuchido's approach could lead to better energy production, reduced inflammation, and overall improvements in health markers associated with aging. Their research has shown significant results, including increases in cellular NAD+ and reductions in biological age, which could benefit those looking to maintain vitality and health as they grow older.

The evidence supporting Nuchido's methods comes from randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled human trials. These studies provide a strong foundation for their claims, distinguishing Nuchido from many companies in the crowded supplement market that often rely on anecdotal evidence. At the upcoming Longevity Show, Nuchido’s founder, Dr. Nichola Conlon, will share insights into their research and how it can translate into practical health strategies for everyday life.

For those eager to explore ways to enhance their healthspan, attending the Longevity Show could be a valuable opportunity. Engaging with experts like Dr. Conlon may offer actionable insights into how to combat age-related decline through lifestyle changes and innovative products.
