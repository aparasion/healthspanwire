Researchers have developed an innovative AI tool called Eko Vet+TM | CANINEBEAT® AI to help veterinarians detect heart murmurs in dogs more effectively. This new technology can identify murmurs that are often missed during routine check-ups, allowing for earlier diagnosis and treatment of heart disease. The CANINEBEAT® AI algorithm has shown over 95% sensitivity and specificity in detecting murmurs related to structural heart disease, which is crucial since heart disease affects about 10% of dogs.

For dog owners, this advancement means that your furry friend could receive timely medical attention for heart issues that might otherwise go unnoticed. Early detection can lead to better management of conditions like myxomatous mitral valve disease (MMVD), a common heart issue that can severely impact your dog's health. By catching these problems sooner, you could help your dog maintain a better quality of life and potentially extend their lifespan.

The Eko Vet+TM system is built on extensive research, having been validated using over 4,000 heart sound recordings from more than 3,400 dogs. While this technology is promising, it is still in the early stages of implementation and is currently being rolled out in the U.S., U.K., and soon in Germany. It's important to note that while the AI tool enhances detection, it does not replace the need for a comprehensive cardiac evaluation by a veterinarian.

As a dog owner, staying informed about your pet's health is vital. If you notice any changes in your dog's behavior or health, consult your veterinarian about the possibility of heart disease, especially if your dog is older or has a breed predisposed to heart issues. Early discussions can help ensure your pet receives the best care possible.
