Centurion Service Group has announced a significant online auction to liquidate medical and non-medical assets from the closure of Fromer Eye Centers, a well-known ophthalmology provider in New York City. Scheduled from May 5 to May 7, this auction will offer a wide range of high-quality ophthalmology and optometry equipment, including advanced surgical machines and diagnostic devices. This event presents a unique opportunity for healthcare providers and equipment resellers to acquire top-tier medical equipment at competitive prices, potentially saving them substantial costs.

For individuals and organizations interested in enhancing their medical capabilities, this auction could mean access to state-of-the-art tools that improve patient care and operational efficiency. The equipment available ranges from fully equipped exam lanes to advanced surgical microscopes, which can significantly benefit ophthalmologists and surgery centers looking to upgrade their facilities. With hundreds of items on offer, bidders can find valuable assets that might otherwise be out of reach financially.

The auction is backed by Centurion’s extensive experience in the medical equipment lifecycle and Auction Advisors' marketing expertise, ensuring a smooth buying process. While the auction is expected to attract strong participation, it's important to note that this is a liquidation sale, and the equipment is being sold as-is. Interested buyers are encouraged to register early for access to the full catalog and to schedule an on-site inspection on May 4 to evaluate the equipment firsthand.
