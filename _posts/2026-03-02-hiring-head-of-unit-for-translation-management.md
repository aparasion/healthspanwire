The European Committee of the Regions (CoR) is actively seeking a Head of Unit for Translation Management, a senior role within its Brussels-based Directorate for Translation. This Directorate provides essential translation services to both the CoR and the European Economic and Social Committee, and the new position will oversee operational processes across its ten units and 24 language sectors. Applications are open until March 5, 2026.

The Head of Unit will be responsible for administrative coordination, resource management, and ensuring the quality of translation services. The role also includes representing the Directorate in IT and technology-related projects and inter-institutional committees. Candidates must possess managerial experience, strong organizational skills, and proficiency in at least two EU languages, with familiarity in translation technology considered advantageous.

This vacancy follows recent recruitment efforts for senior language services roles at other EU institutions, highlighting the ongoing demand for leadership in translation management within the EU framework. For more details on the application process and eligibility criteria, please visit the Committee of the Regions’ website.
