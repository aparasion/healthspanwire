Xpress Wellness has appointed **Joe Willey** as Chief Operating Officer, effective April 27, 2026. Willey will be responsible for overseeing daily operations across Xpress Wellness and Integrity Urgent Care, with a focus on driving operational consistency and enhancing the experience for both team members and patients. His extensive background includes leadership roles at AmeriVet and over three decades at Walgreens, where he honed his skills in managing large, people-centered organizations.

This appointment is particularly significant as Xpress Wellness aims to expand access to high-quality healthcare in rural and suburban communities. Willey’s leadership is expected to strengthen operational frameworks that ensure **consistent, high-quality care** across all locations, aligning with the organization’s mission of patient-first service. His experience in managing growth through new site development and operational integration will be crucial as the organization navigates its next phase of expansion.

The implication of Willey’s appointment extends beyond operational management; it signals a strategic emphasis on enhancing patient care and operational efficiency in underserved areas. This leadership transition may accelerate the timeline for initiatives aimed at expanding access to care, potentially influencing future research and development in healthcare delivery models. As Xpress Wellness continues to grow, the focus on a **people-first approach** could serve as a model for other organizations striving to improve health outcomes in similar communities.
