The GALA Barometer: Economic Indicators is a vital industry survey aimed at providing organizations in the global language sector with actionable insights into their business performance compared to market trends. This initiative addresses the ongoing challenges faced by language service companies and enterprise globalization teams in making informed decisions regarding pricing, staffing, and technology.

The survey collects data on key performance indicators (KPIs), pricing strategies, and revenue benchmarks, allowing participants to understand their alignment with industry standards. Designed for professionals familiar with business operations, the survey takes only 5–10 minutes to complete and ensures that individual responses remain confidential.

In return for their participation, respondents gain access to exclusive benchmarking data and can receive their own results for year-over-year performance comparison. The GALA Barometer is a community-driven resource supported by a dedicated team and a global network, making it an essential tool for informed decision-making in the language industry.
