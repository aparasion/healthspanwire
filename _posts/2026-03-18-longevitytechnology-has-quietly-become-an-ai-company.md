Longevity.Technology has launched a groundbreaking AI-native data infrastructure, Decoding Longevity Trends (DLT), designed specifically for the aging and age-related disease sectors. This innovative platform combines a three-stage AI system with a robust SaaS layer that transforms raw data into actionable intelligence, catering to investors, founders, and pharma business development professionals.

The significance of DLT lies in its ability to streamline the collection and validation of critical data on over 700 longevity biotechs, enhancing decision-making in investment and M&A activities. By employing an agentic AI discovery engine and an independent LLM for fact-checking, DLT ensures high-fidelity insights that surpass general-purpose AI models in accuracy and relevance, making it an essential tool for navigating the rapidly evolving longevity landscape.
