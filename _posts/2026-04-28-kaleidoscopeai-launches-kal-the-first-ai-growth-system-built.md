A new AI tool called KAL™ is set to change how orthodontic practices attract new patients. Launched by KaleidoscopeAI™ at the American Association of Orthodontists Annual Session, this AI Growth System helps orthodontists improve their visibility in a digital landscape increasingly dominated by artificial intelligence. As more people turn to AI platforms like ChatGPT and Google AI for recommendations, traditional search engines are becoming less relevant. KAL™ aims to ensure that orthodontic practices are seen by these AI systems, which now provide answers to healthcare queries more frequently than any other industry.

This development is crucial for anyone seeking orthodontic care. If you’re looking for braces or other dental services, the practices that adapt to this new AI-driven recommendation system will likely be the ones you see first. KAL™ evaluates a practice's online presence across 32 checkpoints, giving it an AI Visibility Score from 0 to 100. Unfortunately, most orthodontic practices currently score below 40, meaning they risk being overlooked by potential patients who rely on AI for recommendations.

The KAL™ system is in its early stages but shows promise for orthodontists wanting to enhance their digital footprint. By using KAL™, practices can better position themselves in front of prospective patients who are increasingly using AI to find providers. Orthodontists attending the AAO Annual Session can receive a complimentary AI Visibility Audit to assess their current standing, while others can request an audit online. This could be a game-changer for practices looking to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital environment.
