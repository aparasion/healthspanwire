In the latest episode of the Boomers Today podcast, host Frank Samson engages with Dr. Adam Solomon to address a pressing issue in the longevity field: the impending shortage of primary care physicians coinciding with an aging population. As the demand for healthcare services increases, this shortage presents significant challenges for the delivery of care to SuperAgers and older adults.

The discussion highlights innovative approaches to primary care that aim to improve patient outcomes and efficiency. Dr. Solomon emphasizes the importance of patient-first care and collaborative healthcare partnerships, exploring what strategies are currently effective and what areas still require improvement. This conversation is particularly relevant for longevity professionals who are navigating the complexities of aging care and seeking solutions to enhance healthspan.

For those invested in the future of healthcare delivery, this podcast episode offers valuable insights into the evolving landscape of primary care and its implications for aging populations.
