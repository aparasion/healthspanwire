Lumina Therapy Alliance has expanded its network with the inclusion of Wise Mind Institute, led by Dr. Wes Pederson, enhancing mental health service accessibility in Silicon Valley. This addition marks Lumina's third practice in the Bay Area, strategically positioned to cater to high-performing individuals, particularly within the tech and legal sectors. Wise Mind Institute is recognized for its tailored approach, combining psychological treatment with executive coaching to optimize the performance of leaders and teams.

The significance of this partnership lies in Lumina's **white-glove concierge model**, which prioritizes direct, relationship-driven care over traditional employee assistance programs (EAPs). This model allows executives to access care within 72 hours, ensuring personalized clinician matching and continuity of care. The integration of psychotherapy, psychiatry, and executive coaching is designed to address not only immediate mental health needs but also the underlying organizational challenges that can lead to burnout and reduced productivity. Dr. Pederson emphasizes that effective leadership development is critical for fostering resilient teams, as many performance issues stem from deeper leadership breakdowns.

This collaboration shifts the paradigm in mental health support for executives, underscoring the importance of **in-person engagement and continuity of care** in high-stakes environments. As Lumina continues to scale its national footprint, the focus on personalized, human-centered care positions it as a compelling alternative to app-based platforms. This approach not only enhances individual well-being but also aligns with organizational performance goals, setting a new standard for mental health services tailored to the unique demands of senior leadership.
