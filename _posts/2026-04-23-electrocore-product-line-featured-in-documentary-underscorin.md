**electroCore, Inc.** has announced its participation in the documentary **Hacking the Vagus Nerve**, produced by The Root Cause Project and The Vagus Nerve Society. This film highlights the vagus nerve's critical role in regulating stress, mood, and immune function, featuring insights from Dr. Peter S. Staats, electroCore’s Chief Medical Officer. The documentary aims to broaden awareness of **non-invasive vagus nerve stimulation (nVNS)** as a therapeutic modality across various health conditions, showcasing electroCore’s technologies like **gammaCore** for migraines and **Truvaga Plus** for stress management.

The significance of this initiative lies in its potential to elevate the understanding of the vagus nerve as a pivotal regulator of multiple physiological systems. The film combines patient narratives with clinical expertise to illustrate how nVNS can effectively support nervous system regulation. With ongoing research and development, electroCore's technologies are positioned to address conditions such as **post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)** and **Long COVID**, emphasizing the growing body of evidence supporting the therapeutic applications of vagus nerve stimulation.

The inclusion of electroCore in this documentary represents a shift in the perception of bioelectronic therapies within the clinical landscape. As interest in nVNS continues to rise, this initiative not only enhances public understanding but also accelerates the integration of vagus nerve modulation into therapeutic protocols. This could potentially shorten drug development timelines and foster new research avenues focused on neuromodulation as a treatment strategy for a range of stress-related disorders.
