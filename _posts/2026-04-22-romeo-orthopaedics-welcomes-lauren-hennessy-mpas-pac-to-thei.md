**Romeo Orthopaedics expands its expertise with the addition of Lauren Hennessy, MPAS, PA-C, enhancing its focus on shoulder and elbow care.** Hennessy, who began her role on April 6, 2026, brings nearly two years of specialized experience in orthopaedic surgery. Her responsibilities include patient evaluation, treatment plan development, perioperative care coordination, and administering joint and soft tissue injections aimed at pain management and functional restoration.

The integration of Hennessy into the team is significant for the clinic's approach to patient-centered care. Her dual background in clinical practice and athletics—having played Division II lacrosse and ice hockey—enables her to address sports-related injuries with a nuanced understanding of the biomechanics involved. This combination of skills aligns with the clinic's mission to provide comprehensive treatment for both elite athletes and everyday patients, particularly those managing chronic pain and seeking to regain mobility.

The key takeaway from Hennessy’s addition is the potential for enhanced patient outcomes through a multidisciplinary approach. Her expertise not only supports the clinic's existing capabilities but also reinforces the importance of integrating athletic experience into clinical practice. This shift could influence future research directions in orthopaedics, particularly in developing targeted interventions for sports-related injuries, ultimately impacting timelines for therapeutic advancements in the field.
