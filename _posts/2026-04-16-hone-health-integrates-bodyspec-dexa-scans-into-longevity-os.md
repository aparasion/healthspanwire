Hone Health has partnered with BodySpec to enhance its personalized operating system for longevity by integrating **clinical-grade DEXA body composition scans**. This collaboration allows patients to purchase DEXA scans directly through the Hone Patient app, with results securely synced to the Hone platform. A Hone clinician then reviews the findings, which include critical metrics such as body composition, bone density, visceral fat, and resting metabolic rate, to inform individualized longitudinal care plans.

This integration is significant as it shifts the paradigm of body composition screening from a post-65 osteoporosis focus to an earlier intervention model. By leveraging DEXA scans, which have been performed over **600,000 times** by BodySpec, Hone aims to provide a more proactive approach to health management. The detailed insights gained from these scans can facilitate targeted interventions that may improve healthspan and longevity outcomes, particularly in identifying and addressing issues related to visceral fat and muscle mass that are critical in aging populations.

The key takeaway from this development is its potential to **accelerate the integration of imaging and biomarker data** into personalized health strategies. This approach not only enhances patient engagement through the app but also supports clinicians in creating more comprehensive and data-driven care plans. As the field moves towards more integrated health solutions, this partnership exemplifies how technology can be harnessed to refine therapeutic strategies and potentially improve patient outcomes in longevity science.
