Eli Lilly has made it clear that it does not have any relationship with Mangoceuticals, Inc. This statement comes after reports falsely suggested a partnership between the two companies. In fact, Eli Lilly previously took legal action against Mangoceuticals and secured a permanent injunction to prevent any misleading claims.

For people looking to manage their health, especially in the realm of prescription medications, it's essential to understand the credibility of the sources and companies involved. Eli Lilly is known for its medications, including Zepbound (tirzepatide), which can be prescribed by healthcare providers. However, it's crucial to ensure that any prescriptions come from legitimate healthcare professionals and are fulfilled through authorized channels.

The situation highlights the importance of being informed about the medications you are prescribed and the companies that produce them. With the rise of telehealth services, it’s easier than ever to access prescriptions, but you should always verify that the healthcare provider is legitimate and that the medications are sourced from approved pharmacies. 

In summary, Eli Lilly is distancing itself from Mangoceuticals and emphasizes that any claims of a partnership are false. This serves as a reminder to stay vigilant about your health information and the providers you choose to trust.
