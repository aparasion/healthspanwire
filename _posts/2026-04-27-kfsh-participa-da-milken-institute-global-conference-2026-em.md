The **King Faisal Specialist Hospital & Research Centre (KFSH)** will serve as a Silver Sponsor at the upcoming **Milken Institute Global Conference 2026** in Beverly Hills, California. This conference aims to unite global leaders from health, finance, technology, policy, and academia to address pressing challenges through innovation, investment, and cross-sector collaboration. Dr. **Björn Zoëga**, Vice-CEO of KFSH, will participate in a session focused on the future of cancer treatment, emphasizing the need for integrated, scalable, and outcome-oriented oncology models.

The significance of KFSH's involvement lies in its commitment to evolving healthcare systems beyond isolated advancements. Dr. Zoëga will advocate for a transformative approach that prioritizes **early detection**, **rapid intervention**, and **continuous learning** in cancer care. This aligns with the broader goal of translating innovation into real-world clinical impact, ensuring that advancements in technology and capital are effectively integrated into patient care to enhance outcomes on a large scale.

This engagement at the Milken Institute conference signals a shift in research paradigms, particularly in oncology. By fostering dialogue among high-level decision-makers across sectors, KFSH is positioned to accelerate the development and adoption of next-generation care models. This could potentially shorten drug development timelines and enhance the scalability of effective treatments, ultimately benefiting patient populations worldwide. The recognition of KFSH as a leading institution in the Middle East and globally underscores its pivotal role in shaping the future of healthcare innovation and collaboration.
