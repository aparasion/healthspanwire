Researchers have opened registration for the 2026 Shoppers Drug Mart® Run for Women, a nationwide event aimed at supporting women’s mental health. This run/walk series, Canada’s largest of its kind, has raised over $27 million since its inception in 2013. Participants can choose between a 5km or 10km route, whether they want to walk or run, and are encouraged to fundraise for local mental health charities that provide essential services like crisis counseling and peer support.

This event is significant for anyone interested in improving mental health and well-being. By participating, you contribute to programs that enhance access to mental health care for women in your community. The funds raised directly support initiatives aimed at reducing stigma and providing critical resources to those in need. Whether you join solo or create a team with friends and family, your involvement can lead to a healthier, more supportive environment for women facing mental health challenges.

The Shoppers Drug Mart® Run for Women is a community-focused initiative with events scheduled in 18 cities across Canada, starting in May 2026. While the event has a strong track record of fundraising, it is still early in the planning stages for this year’s run. Your participation can make a tangible difference, but it’s important to note that the impact of the funds raised will depend on community involvement and support.

To get involved, you can register for an event in your area or consider donating to support women’s mental health initiatives. Every contribution helps build a future where all women in Canada can access the care they deserve.
