SIRIO Europe has launched **Aeion**, a groundbreaking "habit-first" longevity platform at Vitafoods Europe 2026, designed to transform complex aging science into straightforward daily rituals. This initiative addresses the persistent gap between scientific innovation in healthy aging and consumer adoption, emphasizing that sustainable health practices are built on consistent, manageable actions rather than perfection. As SIRIO's Sales Director, Alex Dens, noted, the platform aims to simplify the commercialization of effective supplements, making them accessible and trustworthy for health-conscious consumers.

The significance of Aeion lies in its focus on **behavioral adherence** rather than merely biological efficacy. By offering eight ready-to-market supplement concepts that target common aging concerns—such as energy, mobility, and inflammation—Aeion prioritizes user experience. Formats like gummies, softgels, and dissolvable powders are designed to minimize friction in daily use, thereby enhancing the likelihood of habit formation. For instance, the **Aeion NAD+ Energy** supplement aims to support cellular energy maintenance, while **Aeion Quercetin** provides a convenient solution for managing oxidative stress through a palatable gummy format. This approach aligns with the understanding that longevity solutions must integrate seamlessly into everyday life to be effective.

The broader implication of Aeion's launch is a paradigm shift in the longevity sector towards **habit formation** as a critical driver of healthspan. By prioritizing practical, enjoyable rituals over complex regimens, SIRIO Europe is positioning itself to lead in the emerging market of consumer-friendly longevity products. This shift underscores the importance of developing solutions that resonate with consumers’ lifestyles, ultimately suggesting that the future of longevity research and product development will hinge on creating accessible, repeatable practices rather than solely focusing on scientific breakthroughs.
