Progyny, Inc. has launched **Progyny Select**, a groundbreaking fully insured supplemental fertility and family building plan tailored for small employers in the U.S. This initiative aims to address the rising infertility rates and the increasing demand for fertility coverage, enabling approximately **50 million employees** at small firms to access comprehensive fertility solutions through a fixed premium model. Progyny's CEO, Pete Anevski, emphasizes that this offering democratizes family building, making it a standard benefit rather than a privilege.

The significance of Progyny Select lies in its potential to transform the landscape of fertility benefits by providing a **value-based care model** that has already demonstrated improved clinical outcomes, including faster conception and healthier pregnancies. By extending access to a managed provider network, Smart Cycle benefits, and personalized navigation, Progyny Select supports all paths to parenthood, including IVF, IUI, and genetic testing. This comprehensive approach not only addresses financial and structural barriers but also enhances overall women's health from pregnancy through menopause.

The introduction of Progyny Select could reshape the competitive landscape for small employers, offering them a **strategic advantage** in talent acquisition and retention. As firms like Morris James LLP recognize the importance of fostering a supportive environment for family building, the availability of such benefits may shift the current paradigm in employee health offerings. This could accelerate timelines for drug development and research in reproductive health, as increased demand for fertility solutions may drive innovation and investment in this critical area of women's health.
