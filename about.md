## About HealthspanWire

HealthspanWire covers the science of living longer and staying healthy — written for people who care about their health and want to know what research actually says, without needing a PhD to understand it.

We follow aging biology, longevity treatments, nutrition science, and the technologies that may one day change how we age. For each development, we ask the same questions: What was studied? How strong is the evidence? What does it mean for a real person?

Our goal is simple: signal over noise. Less hype, more honesty.

## Why This Exists

Most longevity coverage either oversells early-stage research or buries the most interesting findings in jargon. HealthspanWire exists to do neither.

We track the most important claims in the field — and tell you honestly where the evidence stands. Whether a finding comes from a mouse study or a large human trial makes a real difference in how seriously you should take it. We always tell you which is which.

> Honest coverage of what the science says — and what it doesn't say yet.

## What We Cover

- **Treatments & Drugs** — drugs, supplements, and therapies being studied to extend healthy life
- **Measuring Aging** — how scientists measure biological age, and what that tells us
- **Food & Lifestyle** — what to eat, how to move, and daily habits that affect how you age
- **Science & Tech** — AI, gene therapy, and tools that may transform how we treat aging
- **Industry & Access** — funding, regulation, and when new treatments might reach you
